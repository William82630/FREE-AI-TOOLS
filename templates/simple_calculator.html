Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the calculator?

Click the buttons or use your keyboard to enter numbers and operations. Press = or Enter to calculate.

What are the memory buttons for?

MC clears memory, MR recalls memory, M+ adds to memory, and M- subtracts from memory.

Is my data safe?

Yes, all calculations are done in your browser and never sent to a server.
