Q 1. What is GST?

A: GST, or Goods and Services Tax, is a comprehensive indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services in India. Implemented on July 1, 2017, GST replaced multiple indirect taxes like VAT, service tax, and excise duty, streamlining the taxation system. It's a destination-based tax, meaning it's collected at the point of consumption rather than the point of origin. GST is categorized into CGST (Central GST), SGST (State GST), IGST (Integrated GST), and UTGST (Union Territory GST), depending on the nature of the transaction.
