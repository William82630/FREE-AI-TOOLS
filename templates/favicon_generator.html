Frequently Asked Questions

What is a favicon?

A favicon is a small icon that represents your website, typically displayed in the browser tab or bookmarks.

What image formats are supported?

Our tool supports PNG, JPG, and JPEG formats for favicon generation.

Is my uploaded image secure?

Yes, all uploaded images are processed securely and are not stored on our servers.
