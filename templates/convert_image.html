What image formats can I convert?
Our tool supports conversion between popular image formats, including PNG, JPG, and JPEG. This ensures compatibility across various platforms and applications, making it easy to prepare your images for websites, social media, or professional projects.

When converting to PNG, there is no quality loss as it uses lossless compression. When converting to JPG/JPEG, there may be slight quality reduction due to the lossy compression nature of these formats.
