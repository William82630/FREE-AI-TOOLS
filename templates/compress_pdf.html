How does PDF compression work?

Our PDF compression tool uses several techniques to reduce file size: optimizing images within the PDF, removing redundant information, compressing text and objects, and restructuring the document's internal structure. The exact methods used depend on the compression level you select.

Will compression affect the quality of my PDF?

The impact on quality depends on the compression level you choose. Low compression preserves nearly all quality while offering modest size reduction. Medium compression provides a good balance between size and quality. High compression yields the smallest file size but may noticeably reduce image quality. Text and vector graphics typically remain sharp at all compression levels.

Is there a file size limit for compression?

Yes, our free compressor has a file size limit of 50MB per PDF. This limit accommodates most standard documents, including reports, presentations, and forms. If you need to compress larger files, consider splitting your PDF into smaller parts before uploading.
