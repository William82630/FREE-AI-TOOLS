Frequently Asked Questions

Why would I need to convert MP4 to MP3?

Converting MP4 to MP3 is useful when you want to extract audio from videos for listening on the go, creating music playlists from video content, reducing file size for storage, using audio clips in presentations or podcasts, or when you only need the audio portion of a video file.

What happens to the video content during conversion?

During the MP4 to MP3 conversion process, the video content (visual elements) is removed, and only the audio track is preserved. This significantly reduces the file size while maintaining the original audio quality based on your selected settings.

Is there a file size limit for conversion?

Yes, our free converter has a file size limit of 1GB per MP4 file. This generous limit accommodates most standard videos, including music videos, movies, presentations, and longer recordings. For extremely large files over 1GB, consider trimming your video into smaller segments before uploading.

How secure is my data during the conversion process?

Our converter offers maximum security because your files never leave your device! The entire conversion process happens directly in your browser using advanced web technology. Your MP4 files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and confidentiality.

Which audio quality should I choose?

The choice depends on your needs. High quality (320 kbps) provides the best sound experience but creates larger files. Medium quality (192 kbps) offers a good balance between quality and file size. Low quality (128 kbps) creates the smallest files, suitable for mobile devices or when storage space is limited.

Can I convert other video formats to MP3?

Currently, our tool is optimized for MP4 to MP3 conversion. For other video formats like AVI, MOV, or WMV, you may need to first convert them to MP4 format using our other conversion tools, then use this tool to extract the audio as MP3.
