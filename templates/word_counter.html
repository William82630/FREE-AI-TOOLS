Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the word count?

Our word counter tool counts words by identifying groups of characters separated by spaces or punctuation. This provides an accurate count for most standard writing, though specialized formats like code may yield different results than expected.

How is the reading time calculated?

The reading time is calculated based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute, which is a commonly accepted average for adults reading content in English. The actual reading time may vary based on the complexity of the text and the reader's speed.

Does the tool count hyphenated words as one word or two?

Hyphenated words (like "state-of-the-art") are counted as a single word, which is consistent with most word counting standards.

Is my text saved or stored anywhere?

No, your text is processed entirely in your browser and is not saved or stored on our servers. When you close the page, your text is gone, ensuring your privacy.

Can I use this tool for academic papers or assignments?

Yes, this tool is perfect for checking the word count of academic papers, essays, or assignments that have specific length requirements. The detailed statistics can also help you analyze and improve your writing style.
